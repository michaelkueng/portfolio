Frequently Asked Questions

01. How long is the photography session?

The minimum time for a photo session is 30 minutes. But it depends on the type of photography session and the package of additional services you have chosen. Any photography session can be extended for an extra fee. But the additional time for the photo session must be specified when ordering.

02. Do I receive all images you shot?

For each package of services, there is a specified number of processed images that you will receive during the photo session. This amount can be increased at an additional cost. You can also order an SD card with all the images from the photo shoot in raw form with an extra price.

03. Do you provide a videography service?

Yes, our studio provides videography services. You can order from us both a separate videography session and in addition to the photo session. This is also a good choice for love stories and wedding photography. Besides, it is possible to order a backstage of your photo shoot.

04. Do you photograph pets?

Unfortunately, at the moment, we don't provide such a service.

05. Do you shoot corporate events or private parties?

There is no single answer to all cases. You can get in touch with us, and we will try to find a solution for your situation individually. So, don't waste your time!

06. What about newborn shoots, family, or individual portraits?

Yes, we do all of this and much more. You can feel free to get in touch with us for further information.

07. Can you provide full day coverage?

Yes, we are ready to work with you all day long. The price for an extra hour started from $150. But many individual points can affect the price, so it is better to contact us to clarify all the details.
